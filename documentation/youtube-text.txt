
ImageSonifyer is a Max application for creating sonomotiongrams. A sonomotiongram is a sonic display of motion from a video recording, created by sonifying a motiongram. A motiongram is a spatiotemporal display of the motion in a video recording.

Created by ImageSonifyer: http://www.uio.no/english/research/groups/fourms/software/imagesonifyer/index.html
